Services

CRE8 PR provides end-to-end engineering design and prototyping services. Engagements can start from either a defined solution idea or an open-ended problem statement.

Typical engagements run approximately 2–6 weeks, depending on scope, complexity, and iteration requirements.

Concept Development

We translate your idea or problem into a clear engineering concept, identifying constraints, use cases, and success criteria.

CAD & Engineering Design

Parametric 3D models and assemblies designed with real-world manufacturing, tolerances, and materials in mind.

Prototyping

Rapid prototyping via 3D printing or simple fabrication to validate form, fit, and function.

Iteration & Refinement

Designs are refined based on physical testing and user feedback until the solution meets performance and usability goals.

Small-Batch Manufacturing

Once validated, we assist with short production runs or transition the design to a manufacturing partner.

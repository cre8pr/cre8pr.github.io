Our
Services

End-to-End

Engagements can start from either a defined solution idea or an open-ended problem statement. Typical projects run 2–6 weeks depending on scope and iteration.

01

Concept Development

We translate your idea or problem into a clear engineering concept — identifying constraints, use cases, and what success actually looks like before a single line is drawn.

~1 WEEK
02

CAD & Engineering Design

Parametric 3D models and assemblies designed with real-world manufacturing, tolerances, and materials in mind. Not just pretty renders — files that can actually be built.

~1–2 WEEKS
03

Rapid Prototyping

Physical prototypes via 3D printing or fabrication to validate form, fit, and function fast. We get something in your hands before assumptions become expensive mistakes.

~1 WEEK
04

Iteration & Refinement

Designs refined based on physical testing and real feedback. We test, break things on purpose, and improve until the solution actually works.

~1–3 WEEKS
05

Small-Batch Manufacturing

Once validated, we assist with short production runs or transition the design to a manufacturing partner with clean, production-ready files and documentation.
